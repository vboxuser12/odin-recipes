Lasagna

Description

This popular italian dish is quite easy to make and absolutely delicious

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • basil
  • cheese
  • lasagna pasta
  • ground beef

    • Steps

    1. Cook beef in pot for 10 minutes or until brown
    2. Add tomatoes to beef and stew
    3. Cook lasagna pasta in pot for 10 minutes
    4. Place one sheet of lasagna on cooking plate then add one layer of sauce and cheese
    5. Cook in oven at 350 for 30 minutes
